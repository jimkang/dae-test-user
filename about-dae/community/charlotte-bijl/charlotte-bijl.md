Charlotte Bijl is freelance projectmanager and takes care of the financial/business management of different cultural organizations. She studied Cultural Heritage at the Reinwardt Academie in Amsterdam, where she minored in Cultural Entrepreneurship. In addition to her role as coordinator of the GEO-Design department at the Design Academy, she works mostly for TAAK, an organization that researches, develops and realizes new forms of art in the public domain and alternative ways of commissioning. As an inquisitive interlocutor, she likes to engage with/join initiatives in health care, spatial planning, ecology, education, science, art and culture, landscape and heritage.

For questions on the schedule, dates, locations, and other matters, contact <charlotte.bijl@designacademy.nl>.
